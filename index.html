Sogndal International conference - 11-17 September 2016

Sogndal International conference

Sogndal international Conference is the ultimate conference that will take place in an environment that favours mutual learning, networking, sight seeing and entertainment while exposing audiences from all over the world to the most pressing issues around the theme of Climate Change, Migration and Integration while surrounded by the beautiful landscape in Sogn og Fjordane.

Program

The conference combines concepts that include: workshops, panel discussions, modules and awards.
It is composed of three modules and a final conference based on the following topics.

1 Climate change
2 Migration
3 Integration

Each workshop lasts for a full day composed of two sessions

Morning session : Interactive lectures

Afternoon session: Parallel workshops

1 Research seminars (Participants present their research followed by a discussion animated by a scholar)
2 Abstract development (participants discuss their abstract in small groups)
3 Field visits (participants visit and perform on site observations in small groups)

Entertainment

An ultimate grand concert will take place on the opening and closing ceremony as well as exciting parties and theme events at the hottest venues in the region which happen every night of the conference, and invites the general public to mingle with the participants. Venues to be announced soon.
