Sogndal International conference - Jyly 30th- August 6th 2017

Sogndal International conference

Sogndal International Youth Conference is the ultimate conference that will take place in an environment that favours mutual learning, networking, sight-seeing and entertainment while exposing young audiences from all over the world to the most pressing issues around the theme of Climate Change, surrounded by the beautiful landscape in Sogn og Fjordane, Western Norway. The aim of the conference is cultural, political and exchange of knowledge on global climate change issues.
Dates: Sunday July 30th - Sunday August 6th.

About us

The conference aims at providing the young from 10-17 as well as young adults with a platform to discuss global issues on an annual basis giving them a chance to participate in educative and cultural exchange goals at national and international levels.

SIYC collaborates with different partners regionally, nationally and internationally including institutions of learning in order to broaden a shared experience. All activities shall focus on the conference theme with an opportunity to visit various organizations and on site activities in order to gain first hand knowledge rounded up by excursions and career fairs.

The conference aims at achieving;

  • Cultural exchanges, between Norwegian, foreign students and professors.
  • Workshops with resolution papers and projects that can be implemented in different countries.
  • Recruiting sessions to bridge the gap between students/graduates and job opportunities at the international level
  • Study abroad exchanges
  • Tourism promotion of the Sogn og Fjordane region.
  • Career fairs
  • Sports and entertainment

Board

The Executive and Advisory Board plays a pivotal role in ensuring strategic planning as well as continuity of the conference over the years by providing advice and any form of support. The board stands particularly at the disposal of the committee to ensure the gathering of experience and knowledge over time is not wasted especially due to the fact that the young generation entails a high turn-over rate which can lead to crucial loss of knowledge.

Workshops

The workshops will be held at different locations (kommunes) within Sogn region. They shall consist of:

  1. Climate Change, Development and Migration
    Has many wars and migrations across the world initially caused by years of drought and reduced food supplies, leading to large scale human migrations?
  2. Climate change, Oil and the energy sector
  3. Climate change sports and mega events
  4. Climate change in the field of information technology
  5. Climate and Health Impacts
  6. Climate change and bio diversity

Social Events

The SIYC will be filled with all sorts of different events providing an opportunity to interact with thousands of people coming from all over the world in a multicultural setting. It is the perfect way to discover the beauty of Sogn og Fjordane region, spend time with newly made friends, an ideal mix of education and entertainment.

Sport

Football- For those passionate about sports, the conference will take place during the biggest international youth football tournaments Lerum cup and Norway cup hence a chance to watch and interact with other sports enthusiasts.
Athletics- Competitive and non-competitive races shall be held during the event as a fun and physical activity.
Tag of war-Team spirit will best be tested with this game.

Projects

Our goal is to create a long standing tradition of nurturing future leaders by encouraging delegates to develop projects before hand that can be introduced at the conference with the best, most applicable project winning grants for implementation in the society. Guidelines
-Delegates can develop projects individually or as a group.
-Project must address a need within the theme of Climate change.
-It should be realistic to achieve and maintain.
